About games

Games are structured forms of play, often undertaken for entertainment, education, or competition. They can be physical or digital, and are designed with rules and objectives that players must navigate to achieve a specific outcome.Games can be classified in two categories online and offline.

Online games

Online games are digital games that can be with or without an internet connection to be played. These can include multiplayer games where players from different locations can interact and compete in real-time, as well as single-player games that rely on online features such as leaderboards or downloadable content. Examples include massively multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPGs), online shooters, and virtual board games.

Physical games

Physical games are activities that involve physical movement and exertion. They're a great way to stay active, have fun, and often involve teamwork or competition.
